# AI Marketing Workshop
 
Free, open-access tools for marketing students and practitioners who want to build real AI skills — not just talk about them.
 
---
 
## Tools
 
### AI Tool Explorer
**[krysd09.github.io/tools](https://krysd09.github.io/tools)**
 
An interactive guide to the AI tools shaping modern marketing. Browse 18+ tools across 7 categories — content, research, analytics, personalisation, SEO, social, and automation. Click any tool to see what it does, who uses it, and how to get started.
 
Built for marketing students who want a practical map of the landscape, not another listicle.
 
---
 
### AI Career Roadmap
**[krysd09.github.io/roadmap](https://krysd09.github.io/tools/roadmap.html)**
 
A personalised 12-month roadmap for building AI skills that employers are actually asking for. Answer a few questions about your background and goals — the tool generates a sequenced learning path with specific skills, tools, and milestones tied to real job posting data.
 
Built for students who want to know what to do next, not just what matters in general.
 
---
 
### Kova Simulation
**[atolilabs.github.io/kova](https://atolilabs.github.io/kova)**
 
A live AI marketing simulation. You play the role of an AI marketing analyst on the US launch team for Kova — a premium sparkling functional tea brand from Singapore entering the American market. Work through five tasks using AI tools: market landscape analysis, consumer segment mapping, competitive analysis, a live marketing dashboard, and a creative brief.
 
Built for students who want a real portfolio piece, not another certificate.
